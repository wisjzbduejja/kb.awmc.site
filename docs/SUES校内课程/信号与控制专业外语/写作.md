### English Version (英文版)

**Welcome to the College of Urban Railway Transportation at SUES**

**About Us**

Located in the dynamic metropolis of Shanghai, the **College of Urban Railway Transportation** at **Shanghai University of Engineering Science (SUES)** is a premier institution dedicated to cultivating high-caliber engineering and management talents for the modern transit industry. Closely aligned with industry needs, our college combines rigorous academic theories with extensive hands-on practice. We are proud to offer state-of-the-art facilities and programs that are recognized by international engineering education accreditations, ensuring our graduates are highly competitive on a global scale.

**Our Undergraduate Programs**

We offer four specialized undergraduate majors, comprehensively covering the core technical and operational domains of the rail transit industry:

- **Vehicle Engineering (Urban Rail Transit Vehicles):** Focuses on the design, manufacturing, testing, and maintenance of modern rail vehicles.
    
- **Rail Transit Signal and Control:** Trains experts in the vital communication, signaling, and automated control systems that guarantee operational safety and efficiency.
    
- **Transportation:** Cultivates professionals in rail transit operation, scheduling, passenger transport organization, and system management.
    
- **Railway Engineering:** Prepares engineers for the design, construction, and maintenance of railway infrastructure, including tracks, bridges, and underground tunnels.
    

**Admissions & Application**

We welcome passionate students who are eager to shape the future of urban mobility to join our community. Domestic students can apply through the National College Entrance Examination (Gaokao) system. For international applicants, please submit your applications through the official SUES international admissions portal. For detailed application guidelines, entry requirements, and important deadlines, please visit the official SUES admissions website or contact our college's admission office directly. Embark on your engineering journey with us!

### 中文对照参考 (Chinese Translation)

**欢迎来到上海工程技术大学城市轨道交通学院**

**关于我们**

位于充满活力的国际大都市上海，**上海工程技术大学（SUES）城市轨道交通学院**是一所致力于为现代轨道交通行业培养高素质工程和管理人才的顶尖学院。我们的学院紧密贴合行业需求，将严谨的学术理论与丰富的实践操作相结合。我们引以为豪的是拥有最先进的设施以及获得国际工程教育认证的课程项目，确保我们的毕业生在全球范围内具备高度竞争力。

**我们的本科专业**

我们提供四个专门的本科专业，全面涵盖了轨道交通行业的核心技术和运营领域：

- **车辆工程（城市轨道交通车辆）：** 专注于现代轨道车辆的设计、制造、测试和维护。
    
- **轨道交通信号与控制：** 培养在通信、信号和自动控制系统方面的专家，以保障运营的安全与高效。
    
- **交通运输：** 培养轨道交通运营、调度、客运组织和系统管理方面的专业人才。
    
- **铁道工程：** 为铁路基础设施（包括轨道、桥梁和地下隧道）的设计、施工和维护培养土木与铁道工程师。
    

**招生与报考**

我们欢迎热衷于塑造城市出行未来的学子加入我们的大家庭。国内学生可通过全国普通高等学校招生统一考试（高考）系统进行报考。对于国际申请者，请通过上海工程技术大学官方国际招生门户网站提交申请。获取详细的报考指南、录取要求和重要截止日期，请访问上海工程技术大学官方招生网站或直接联系我们学院的招生办公室。与我们一起开启您的工程之旅吧！
